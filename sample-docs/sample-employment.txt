EMPLOYMENT CONTRACT

This Employment Contract is made between Pinnacle Tech Ltd (the Employer)
and James Robertson (the Employee).

1. POSITION AND DUTIES
The Employee is appointed to the position of Senior Software Engineer
reporting to the Head of Engineering. The Employee shall perform such
duties as are reasonably assigned by the Employer.

2. COMMENCEMENT DATE
Employment commences on 1 August 2026. The first six months shall
constitute a probationary period during which employment may be
terminated by either party on one weeks notice.

3. SALARY AND BENEFITS
The Employee shall receive a gross annual salary of £85,000 payable
monthly in arrears on the last working day of each month. The Employee
is entitled to 25 days annual leave plus public holidays.

4. WORKING HOURS
Core hours are 9am to 5pm Monday to Friday. The Employee may be
required to work additional hours as reasonably necessary to fulfil
their duties without additional remuneration.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
The Employee shall not during or after employment disclose any
confidential information belonging to the Employer or its clients.

6. RESTRICTIVE COVENANTS
For a period of 12 months following termination the Employee shall not
solicit or employ any member of staff employed by the Employer nor
solicit any client of the Employer with whom they had material dealings.

7. GOVERNING LAW
This contract is governed by the laws of England and Wales.
